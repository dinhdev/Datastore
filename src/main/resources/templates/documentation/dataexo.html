Free and Premium Datasets

Each Featured data is distinguished by an identifier which says if it ‘Premium’ or ‘Free’. They are described and differentiated as below:

  • Free – You can access this data free of cost.

 You may not have complete access to data here and only partial data is available.

 

  • Premium- You can access this data by paying for it.

    This features shows you complete set of data and complete access is granted.

 

Let’s check each of these data in detail:

  • Free data.

Click on the featured data with 'Free' written in front of it and you will be directed to the following page:

 

 

Click on Purchase button and you will be directed to the following page.

Purchase amount is $0  i.e. you need not pay for this data.

 

  • Premium Data

Click on the featured data with Premium written in front of it and you will be directed to the following page:

 

 

You will be able to see only limited or partial dataset series. If you want to see the complete data, please Click PURCHASE. You will be directed to the following page:

As highlighted in red there is a cost attached to this data since its premium. In this case, you need to pay $10 to see the complete data.
